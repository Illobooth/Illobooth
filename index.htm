Welcome to the Illustration Booth, my illustrious, illustrated, online portfolio! My name is David and I'm an illustrator. I also do stuff like graphic and web design but my first love will always be illustration.


The word illustrator is of Latin origin, and means one who enlightens. To enlighten is to shed light on a subject or situation; to impart information or aid understanding, to instruct, edify and free from ignorance, prejudice, or superstition. Illustrations have the power to enlighten people, and this is why I love being an illustrator.


I don't know if I'll ever truly enlighten anyone with my humble doodlings, but I certainly have fun trying. I hope you discover something within this virtual gallery that brings a smile to your face.
